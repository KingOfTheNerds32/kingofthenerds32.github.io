Who Am I

I use machine learning, engineering, and effective communication to deliver insights that have tremendous impact for my clients.

I've spent over a decade building and managing teams and products in the data science and analytics space that specialize in using big data, predictive modeling, and machine learning in order to deliver incredible data-driven impact. During that time working as a data scientist, business analyst, and product manager, I've consulted with hundreds of clients, including 50+ Fortune 500 companies, to answer and solve some of their most challenging business questions.

I also place special emphasis on empowering others to become successful analysts by leading a variety of trainings and workshops for corporate clients and universities including Lieberman Research Worldwide, University of Southern California, and General Assembly.

What I do

I help people design, build, and master technologies that enable them to use their data to achieve exceptional results.

Data Pipelines

Moving data from A to B is the first step in using it to answer your key business questions. I develop systems that move information to different parts of your organization, so that each team can maximize the value they extract from this information.

Data Warehouses

How you store your data is a key factor in your ability to turn this information into impactful insights. I design and build effective data storage solutions for clients using technologies like Amazon Redshift, Amazon Athena, PostgreSQL, Microsoft Azure SQL Database, NoSQL, etc.

Machine Learning

Machine learning, and the larger data science field, can supercharge your ability to drive results. I build and socialize production quality supervised & unsupervised machine learning models that help you understand how & why customers make decisions and enhance your customer's experiences with your company.

Data Visualization

A successful data-driven strategy includes exposing data throughout your organization so that team members can make highly informed decisions. I operationalize and scale real-time dashboards and reporting solutions using technologies like Microsoft PowerBI, Tableau, AWS Quicksight, etc.

Product Management

Designing great products requires a solid technical foundation, data driven problem solving, and a talent for synthesizing customer feedback into tangible action items. I solve customers' pain points by guiding the development of great software informed by the rigorous use of analytics.

Training

Team members need to be empowered to use the tools at their disposal in order to make informed business decisions. I lead bespoke training sessions on everything to do with Analytics including basic Excel skills, Data Analysis, Python, SQL, leveraging predictive models, etc.
